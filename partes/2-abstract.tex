\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Resumen}
\noindent
This work addresses the design and implementation of a web application aimed at automating proforma invoice control at the Confort Muebles factory, located in the city of Cuenca. The identified problem lies in the manual management of proformas, customers, and settlements, which causes recording errors, document duplication, information loss, delays in customer service, and difficulties in sales tracking. In addition, the lack of a centralized database limits timely access to information and affects decision-making. The general objective is to implement a web application that optimizes proforma management and modernizes administrative processes. The specific objectives include analyzing current processes, designing the system architecture, developing the functional modules, and evaluating the impact of the implemented solution. To address this problem, a web application was developed using modern technologies that centralizes information, automates proforma generation, and manages customers, inventory, and settlements, while also generating administrative reports. This solution is expected to reduce working time, minimize errors, improve information control, and strengthen the operational efficiency and competitiveness of the company through digital transformation.

\vspace{0.3cm}
\noindent
\textbf{Keywords:} web application, process automation, proforma management, centralized database, digital transformation.
